Chickpea Salad

Description

This quick and easy lunch is similar to a classic chicken salad, but it's made with mashed chickpeas instead. It is perfect for vegetarians and can easily be made vegan by swapping the mayo for a plant-based alternative.

Packed with protein, fiber, and flavor, this salad is creamy, crunchy, and very satisfying, even when you are short on time. This is a go-to in my house for meal prepping as it stores in the refrigerator for up to a week!

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas - 2 cans, drained and rinsed
  • Celery - 6 stalks, finely chopped
  • Green onion - 2 stalks, finely chopped
  • Fresh dill - 1/2 cup, minced
  • Mayonnaise - 1 cup
  • Dijon mustard - 1 tbs
  • Capers optional - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Lemon juice - to taste
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Place the chickpeas in a large bowl and mash with a fork or potato masher until most of the chickpeas are smashed.
  2. Stir in the celery, green onions, and capers.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining ingredients to create dressing.
  4. Add dressing to the chickpea mixture and stir until well-combined. Add salt and pepper to taste.
